LASHED BY LAM LAM

Hybrid style

Hybrid lashes refer to a type of eyelash extension style that combines both classic and volume lash techniques. In this approach, individual extensions are applied to each natural lash, similar to classic lash extensions, but with the addition of lighter, thinner volume lashes interspersed throughout to create a fuller, textured look.

Volume style

Volume lashes are a type of eyelash extension technique where multiple lightweight (0.07)extensions, typically between 2D-6D, are applied to each natural lash, allowing for the creation of a fuller and more voluminous look without adding excessive weight to the natural lashes.

Wispy designed

Wispy lashes refer to a style of eyelash extensions or false lashes that have a feathery, fluttery appearance, resembling the texture of wisps of hair. These lashes typically feature longer strands at the outer corners, gradually tapering to shorter lengths towards the inner corners. The varying lengths and layers create a soft and romantic look that enhances the eyes with a subtle yet glamorous effect.

Classic style

Classic lashes, also known as individual lashes, are a type of eyelash extension technique where a single extension (0.15) is applied to each natural lash. These extensions are typically thicker and longer than natural lashes, providing length and volume to enhance the appearance of the eyes.Classic lash extensions are applied using a special adhesive that bonds the extension to the natural lash, creating a seamless and natural-looking result.
